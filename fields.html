Pick224 - Field Explanations

  • LEAGUE − The league the player played in during the season
  • NAME − The player's name
  • PLAYER ID − An identifier unique to the specific player
  • POS − Forward or Defense
  • SEASON − The last year of the season - ex: 2018-2019
  • DY+/- − How far removed a player was from their first-time-eligible draft year in the given season. ex: DY-1 means their draft season was/is the following season, DY means this season was their draft season, and DY+1 means their draft season was the previous season
  • # − The player's most recent jersey number
  • GP − Games played
  • EV P1/GP − Even-strength primary points per GP (goals and primary assists)
  • PP P1/GP − Powerplay primary points per GP (goals and primary assists)
  • P1/GP − Total primary points per GP
  • EV G − Even-strength goals
  • EV A1 − Even-strength primary assists
  • EV A2 − Even-strength secondary assists
  • EV P1 − Even-strength primary points (goals and primary assists)
  • PP G − Powerplay goals
  • PP A1 − Powerplay primary assists
  • PP A2 − Powerplay secondary assists
  • PP P1 − Powerplay primary points (goals and primary assists)
  • SH G − Shorthanded goals
  • SH A1 − Shorthanded primary assists
  • SH A2 − Shorthanded secondary assists
  • EV SH − Even-strength shots
  • EV SH% − Even-strength shooting percentage
  • PP SH − Powerplay shots
  • PP SH% − Powerplay shooting percentage
  • SHOTS − Total shots
  • SH% − Total shooting percentage
  • SHOTS/GP − Total shots per GP
  • TG − Total goals
  • TP1 − Total primary points
  • TP − Total points
  • TP/GP − Total points per GP
  • EV% − The percentage of primary points scored at even-strength
  • Goals/P1 − The percentage of primary points that were goals
  • EV GF − The number of even-strength goals scored by the player's team while the player was on the ice
  • EV GA − The number of even-strength goals scored by the other team while the player was on the ice
  • EV GF% − The percentage of even-strength goals scored by the player's team while the player was on the ice
  • EV INV% − The percentage of even-strength goals scored by the player's team while the player was on the ice that the player either scored or got the primary assist on
  • EV GF%Rel − The player's EV GF% minus the percentage of even-strength goals scored by the player's team while the player was not on the ice
  • OFF EV GF − The number of even-strength goals scored by the player's team while the player was not on the ice
  • OFF EV GA − The number of even-strength goals scored by the other team while the player was not on the ice
  • MISSING GF% − Women only: the percentage of the player's games missing data for which players were on the ice for each goal
  • eTOI/GP − Estimated time on ice per game in minutes, calculated using hockey-prospects.com's formulas. Actual TOI figures for Liiga, SHL, Allsvenskan, FinnU20, KHL, and MHL players.
  • P1/e60 − Total primary points per estimated 60 minutes of time on ice
  • DOB − Date of birth
  • AGE SEPT 15 − The player's age on September 15 of the year indicated in SEASON (the NHL draft cutoff)
  • AGE DEC 31 − The player's age on December 31 of the year indicated in SEASON
  • AGE − Women only: the player's age on December 31 of the year indicated in SEASON
  • TEAM − The team or teams the player played for during the season
  • BIRTHPLACE − The player's birthplace, according to Elite Prospects
  • HT − The player's height, according to Elite Prospects at the time the season's data was pulled
  • WT − The player's weight, according to Elite Prospects at the time the season's data was pulled
  • SHOOTS − The player's handedness, if available
  • DRAFT YEAR − The first year the player was eligible to be selected in the NHL draft
  • DRAFT STATUS − Whether the player has been drafted, or their status if not
  • NHL DRAFT − For drafted players, the NHL team that selected them, the year they were drafted, and the pick they were selected with
  • DRAFT TEAM − For drafted players, the NHL team that selected them
  • NHL TEAM − The NHL team that currently owns the player's rights
  • HEATMAP − A density map displaying the positions the player took shots and scored goals from throughout the season
