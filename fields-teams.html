Pick224 - Field Explanations

  • SEASON − The last year of the season - ex: 2018-2019
  • LEAGUE − The specific league
  • TEAM − The team's short code
  • TEAM_LONG − The team's city or location
  • GP − Games played
  • SOG − The team's total shots on goal
  • SOG/G − Shots on goal per game
  • SOGA − The team's total shots on goal against
  • SOGA/G − Shots on goal against per game
  • SOGDIF − SOG/G minus SOGA/G
  • EV GF − Even-strength goals for
  • EV GA − Even-strength goals against
  • PP GF − Powerplay goals for
  • PP GA − Powerplay goals against
  • SH GF − Shorthanded goals for
  • SH GF − Shorthanded goals against
  • EV GF/G − Even-strength goals for per game
  • EV GA/G − Even-strength goals against per game
  • PP GF/G − Powerplay goals for per game
  • PP GA/G − Powerplay goals against per game
  • EVGDIF − EV_GF/G minus EV_GA/G
  • PPGDIF − PP_GF/G minus PP_GA/G
  • SOG% − Shots for as a percentage of shots for plus shots against
  • EV GF% − Even-strength goals for as a percentage of goals for plus goals against
