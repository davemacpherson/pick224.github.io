Pick224 - Field Explanations

  • LEAGUE − The league the player played in during the season
  • NAME − The player's name
  • PLAYER ID − An identifier unique to the specific player
  • SEASON − The last year of the season - ex: 2018-2019
  • DY+/- − How far removed a player was from their first-time-eligible draft year in the given season. ex: DY-1 means their draft season was/is the following season, DY means this season was their draft season, and DY+1 means their draft season was the previous season
  • TEAM − The team or teams the player played for during the season
  • GP − Games played
  • MIN − Minutes played
  • SHOTS − Shots faced, all situations (EV/PP/SH)
  • GOALS − Goals allowed, all situations (EV/PP/SH)
  • SV% − Save percentage, or percentage of shots saved, all situations (EV/PP/SH)
  • GAA − Goals allowed per 60 minutes played, all situations (EV/PP/SH)
  • GSAA − Goals saved above average, all situations (EV/PP/SH)
  • GSAA/60 − Goals saved above average per 60 minutes played, all situations (EV/PP/SH)
  • EV GSAA − Even-strength goals saved above average
  • PP GSAA − Powerplay goals saved above average
  • Q SHOTS − Quality shots faced, all situations (EV/PP/SH)
  • Q GOALS − Goals allowed on quality shots, all situations (EV/PP/SH)
  • Q SV% − Save percentage on quality shots, all situations (EV/PP/SH)
  • NQ SHOTS − Non-quality shots faced, all situations (EV/PP/SH)
  • NQ GOALS − Goals allowed on non-quality shots, all situations (EV/PP/SH)
  • NQ SV% − Save percentage on non-quality shots, all situations (EV/PP/SH)
  • EV SHOTS − Even-strength shots faced
  • EV GOALS − Even-strength goals allowed
  • EV SV% − Even-strength save percentage
  • PP SHOTS − Powerplay shots faced
  • PP GOALS − Powerplay goals allowed
  • PP SV% − Powerplay save percentage
  • EV Q SHOTS − Even-strength quality shots faced
  • EV Q GOALS − Even-strength goals allowed on quality shots
  • EV Q SV% − Save percentage on even-strength quality shots
  • EV NQ SHOTS − Even-strength non-quality shots faced
  • EV NQ GOALS − Even-strength goals allowed on non-quality shots
  • EV NQ SV% − Save percentage on even-strength non-quality shots
  • PP Q SHOTS − Powerplay quality shots faced
  • PP Q GOALS − Powerplay goals allowed on quality shots
  • PP Q SV% − Save percentage on powerplay quality shots
  • PP NQ SHOTS − Powerplay non-quality shots faced
  • PP NQ GOALS − Powerplay goals allowed on non-quality shots
  • PP NQ SV% − Save percentage on powerplay non-quality shots
  • DOB − Date of birth
  • AGE SEPT 15 − The player's age on September 15 of the year indicated in SEASON (the NHL draft cutoff)
  • AGE DEC 31 − The player's age on December 31 of the year indicated in SEASON
  • HT − The player's height at the time the season's data was pulled
  • CATCHES − The hand the goalie wears his catching glove on
  • DRAFT YEAR − The first year the player was eligible to be selected in the NHL draft
  • NHL DRAFT − For drafted players, the NHL team that selected them, the year they were drafted, and the pick they were selected with
  • DRAFT TEAM − For drafted players, the NHL team that selected them
  • NHL TEAM − The NHL team that currently owns the player's rights
